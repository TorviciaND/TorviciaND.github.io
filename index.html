• Click on a card and place it anywhere on the board where there isn't a gray blocker.
  • If you place a card with an arrow pointing to an enemy arrow, the cards will fight.
  • The winning card will "cascade" their allegience to all cards that it points to.
  • If the enemy card does not have an arrow, your card will take it over. Be careful though because all attacks must occur before takeovers, and if you lose a battle, your turn is over.
